Who is Jesus?

Jesus is He who offers all man freedom.

The Lord's teaching tells us that life exists beyond our time on Earth. In our personal relationship with Him, we not only experience authentic life now, but in the life to come.

He gives eternal life. When we follow Jesus, we grow stronger and become more like Him. We should remember Him and follow Him like sunflowers follow the sun across the sky. Following His light and example will bring us joy, happiness, and peace, even if things around us are hard.

Where Jesus Walked

My must visit holy spaces

Mount of Beautitudes

The Mount of Beatitudes is a hill in Northern Israel on the Korazim Plateau. It is the spot where Jesus is believed to have delivered his Sermon on the Mount. Overlooking the northwestern shore of the Sea of Galilee, the mount offers enchanting views of the northern part of the lake and across to the cliffs of the Golan Heights.

Address

Mt. of Beatitudes

Why visit here?

The Mount of Beatitudes, believed to be the setting for Jesus' most famous discourse, the Sermon on the Mount, is one of the most beautifully serene places in the Holy Land.

Nazareth

In this old city, the domed Basilica of the Annunciation is, some believe, where the angel Gabriel told Mary she would bear a child. It's known as the childhood home of Jesus and the largest Arab city in Israel hosting its stunning location right in the middle of the Lower Galilee.

Address

5079 St., Nazareth 16100 Israel

Why visit here?

Here, Jesus learned carpentry and stonework from His father, Joseph. As an adult, He returns to Nazareth and at the synagogue He confesses to be the fulfillment of the prophet Isaiah's words in Luke 4:18-19.

Learn More

Cana of Galilee

Cana is an Arab village nestled in the mountains the lower Galilee. It's an ordinary busy town and you have to walk through small streets to reach the Church of the 1st Miracle where water was turned into wine. This famous miracle in the Village of Cana is recorded in the New Testament.

Address

Kfar Cana, Israel

Why visit here?

It is mentioned in the Bible to be the place of the first miracle of Jesus, who turned water into wine on the occasion of a wedding feast. The Shrine of Jesus' First Miracle located here is under the custody of the Franciscans with presence in Cana for three centuries.
